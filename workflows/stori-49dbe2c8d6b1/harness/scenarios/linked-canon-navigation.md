# Linked Canon Navigation

## Why This Scenario Exists

Trust in canon depends on relationship clarity. Users need to move through the world graph without losing context.

## Representative User

A creator is reviewing a character and needs to understand how that character relates to factions, locations, items, and timeline events.

## Task

Open an entity, inspect its linked canon, and follow relationships across the world without losing the original context.

## Expected Outcome

- related entities are clearly surfaced
- links feel typed and meaningful, not like generic backlinks
- users can move from one entity to another and still understand where they came from
- the graph remains understandable as content volume grows

## Validation Ideas

- start from a character with at least three different relationship types
- follow links into a faction, then into a location, then back to the original character
- verify that each entity page preserves clear relationship context

## Failure Signals

- relationships look like undifferentiated tags
- users cannot tell why two entities are linked
- navigation becomes disorienting after only a few hops
